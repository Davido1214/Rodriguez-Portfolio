My name is David Rodriguez I am an upcoming developer. My current top project is the Run Buddy webpage. I am currently based in Phoenix while learning and practicing html/css/js and more in the near future. My goal in the next 6 months is to prepare myself for a full time job in the field working as a back-end developer or a fullstack developer.

This project is labeled Run Buddy. As in the title states this website is based around running; with a buddy nonetheless. This website was designed to socialize runners with each other. To advocate for exercise; and to be a bridge of data and information for all who welcome it, regardless of having never ran before or being a daily runner.
